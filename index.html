Welcome, traveler. You have found your way to Wyrmwood Palisades — a corner of the realm dedicated to the things that matter: stories told around candlelight, songs that carry you through long roads, and games that demand strategy, luck, and a little dragon fire.

This keep is still being built, stone by stone. The banners are being hung, the torches lit. Soon these halls will hold chronicles, compositions, and campaign logs for any who care to read them.

Pull up a chair. The fire is warm and the night is long.
